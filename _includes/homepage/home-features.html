Decoding campaign finance data

We're helping the California Civic Data Coalition make the state's arcane database tracking money in politics easy to mine -- a project recently awarded $250,000 by Knight Foundation.

Teaching five data journalism classes

At Stanford, we've launched five new courses focused on public affairs reporting, computational methods and investigative journalism. We're also exploring immersive storytelling with virtual reality in a new seminar.

Building a computational journalism community

We're organizing speaker events, launching a Hacks/Hackers at Stanford and hosting the annual Computation + Journalism Symposium in 2016.
