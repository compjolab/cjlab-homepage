Gatherings

Computational Conferences

In Fall 2016, we'll help host the Computation + Journalism Symposium, which will be at Stanford. The annual conference explores the interface between journalism and computing. Previous years were hosted by Columbia in New York.
  • 2015 C+J Symposium
  • 2014 C+J Symposium

Hacks/Hackers

This fall, we'll be launching a Hacks/Hackers chapter at Stanford. The group will host quarterly events on campus that bring journalists ("hacks") and technologists ("hackers") from the Stanford and wider Silicon Valley communities together.
