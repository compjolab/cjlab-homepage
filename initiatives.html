In Fall 2016, we will host the Computation + Journalism Symposium at Stanford. The annual conference explores the interface between journalism and computing.

Previous years:

  • 2015 C+J Symposium
  • 2014 C+J Symposium
We hope to launch a Hacks/Hackers chapter at Stanford. The group will host quarterly events on campus that bring journalists ("hacks") and technologists ("hackers") from the Stanford and wider Silicon Valley communities together.
